Hello World

We are Arden and Tyler, and we met at a Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program during the summer of 2015. We created "What Do You Care About?" because of our passion for politics and self advocacy. "What Do You Care About?" is a tool that helps young activists find outlets for their activism. We help them change the issues that affect them. We have multiple cool aspects of our website. If you live in NYC, we hope you take advantage of our maps detailing the best organizations in the city. But no matter where you live, check out our "Young Activist's Toolkit"! It is a collection of materials that will set you on your path to influencing positive change in your community.
